Instructions:
  • Double-click on a cell to edit. Note only "Modify URL", "Priority", "Display Secs", and "Updated By" are editable. Edits are automatically saved to the database.
  • To attach a new Url to a Channel, click Add Url to Channel button. A new Url row will appear that can be edited. Edits are automatically saved to the database.
  • To delete a Url(s) from a Channel, first select a Url Row(s) by checking the lefthand side checkmark and then click Delete Channel button.
