Instructions:
  • Double-click on a cell to edit. Note only "Channels", "Descriptions", and "Updated By" are editable. Edits are automatically saved to the database.
  • To create a new Channel, click Add Channel button. A new Channel row will appear that can be edited. Edits are automatically saved to the database.
  • To delete a Channel(s), first select a Channel Row(s) by checking the lefthand side checkmark and then click Delete Channel button.
